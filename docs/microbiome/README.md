# Microbiome

## Why This Matters

Your gut microbiome—the trillions of bacteria, viruses, and fungi living in your digestive tract—plays a central role in health and disease. It influences immune function, mental health, metabolism, and disease risk.

Functional medicine views the microbiome not as an isolated system, but as deeply interconnected with nutrition, immune function, hormone metabolism, and systemic inflammation.
